The 16 Personalities

Understanding your Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is the first step to forming the ultimate hackathon team. GoalLink pairs highly complementary working styles so your team balances vision with execution, and logic with empathy.

Analysts (Intuitive & Thinking)

INTJ
The Architect

Imaginative and strategic thinkers, with a plan for everything. Excellent at seeing the big picture systems and creating the initial hackathon vision.

StrategicIndependent
INTP
The Logician

Innovative inventors with an unquenchable thirst for knowledge. Great for solving complex algorithmic bottlenecks in your project.

AnalyticalCurious
ENTJ
The Commander

Bold, imaginative, and strong-willed leaders. The perfect people to manage project timelines, pitch the final product, and delegate tasks.

DrivenOrganized
ENTP
The Debater

Smart and curious thinkers who cannot resist an intellectual challenge. Incredible brainstormers who will pivot and adapt your idea on the fly.

CreativeEnergetic

Diplomats (Intuitive & Feeling)

INFJ
The Advocate

Quiet and mystical, yet very inspiring and tireless idealists. They ensure the product truly helps people and are heavily focused on UI/UX empathy.

InsightfulPassionate
INFP
The Mediator

Poetic, kind and altruistic people, always eager to help a good cause. They build inclusive team cultures and write deeply impactful pitch decks.

EmpatheticCreative
ENFJ
The Protagonist

Charismatic and inspiring leaders, able to mesmerize their listeners. The ultimate hype-person to present the project to hackathon judges.

CharismaticReliable
ENFP
The Campaigner

Enthusiastic, creative and sociable free spirits. They network effectively and think outside the box to bypass technical roadblocks.

EnthusiasticSpontaneous

Sentinels (Observant & Judging)

ISTJ
The Logistician

Practical and fact-minded individuals, whose reliability cannot be doubted. They will write the tests, fix the bugs, and make sure the demo actually works.

ResponsibleDetail-Oriented
ISFJ
The Defender

Very dedicated and warm protectors, always ready to defend their loved ones. They keep team morale high when the 3 AM hackathon crash hits.

SupportivePractical
ESTJ
The Executive

Excellent administrators, unsurpassed at managing things or people. They will enforce Git workflows, merge branches, and keep scope creep strictly in check.

OrganizedDirect
ESFJ
The Consul

Extraordinarily caring, social and popular people. Highly skilled at bringing people together in cross-functional collaboration efforts.

CollaborativeLoyal

Explorers (Observant & Prospecting)

ISTP
The Virtuoso

Bold and practical experimenters, masters of all kinds of tools. Unmatched mechanics at connecting APIs, scraping data, and hacking components together.

LogicalSpontaneous
ISFP
The Adventurer

Flexible and charming artists, always ready to explore and experience something new. The visual geniuses who will make your frontend look beautiful fast.

ArtisticFlexible
ESTP
The Entrepreneur

Smart, energetic and very perceptive people. They thrive on the edge and excel in high-pressure crunch-time situations right before the submission deadline.

BoldPerceptive
ESFP
The Entertainer

Spontaneous, energetic and enthusiastic people. They ensure the hacker environment stays vibrant and fun throughout the grueling development lifecycle.
